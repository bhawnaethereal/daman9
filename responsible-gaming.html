Responsible Gaming

Hello friend, This message is not to stop you. It is just to say – play smart.

Daman game is a game, yes, but it is also real money. So please use it with care.

We want you to enjoy, not regret.

Game Is for Timepass, Not for Stress

Game should make you smile, not make you worry. If you are feeling low, tired or angry, do not open the app. Go outside, talk to someone, take rest.

Do not think this game will solve money problems. Some win, some lose. That is normal.

If you win – good. If you lose – stop. Try later or leave it.

Are You Playing Too Much? Just Think Once

Ask yourself:

  • Are you playing late at night every day?
  • Are you missing study or work?
  • Are you hiding it from family?
  • Are you using money meant for other things?
  • Are you getting angry when you lose?

If even 2 answers are yes, stop and take a break.

Play With Limit – Simple Rules to Follow

  • Fix a time daily. Do not go beyond it.
  • Keep money aside just for play. Small amount.
  • Never take loan or borrow to play.
  • Sleep properly. Eat properly.
  • Keep balance in life. Game is not everything.

No one will stop you. But you must stop yourself when needed.

Daman game Can Help You Too

If you ever feel like you are not able to control, message us. We will:

  • Pause your account if you want
  • Put a daily limit
  • Suggest a cool-off time
  • Answer your questions honestly

We are not here only for gaming. We are here to protect users too.

Parents, Family, Friends – Please Watch Too

If you think someone close to you is playing too much, please talk. No need to fight. Just sit with them. Ask them how they feel. Sometimes just talking makes a big difference.

Your Mind Is More Important Than Any Game

We are saying again – do not chase loss. Do not play with fear. Play only when heart is light.

You are more important than the app. This game will stay. Your health must stay first.

Need Help? We Are Just a Message Away

If you ever feel confused, stuck, or just need a break:

Email us at: info@damangameapp.co

We will reply with full respect.

Final Line – From Our Side to Yours

Thank you for reading this. Please enjoy Daman game. But enjoy with care. Play like a game. Live like a winner. Stay safe. Stay happy.
